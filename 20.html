주어(I) + could not + 동사 : 나는 + ~할수 없었어/못했어

  • I could not/couldn't
  • wash my face.
  • wash my face yesterday.
  • make coffee.
  • make coffee yesterday.
  • eat breakfast.
  • eat breakfast then.
  • drink juice.
  • drink juice then.
  • clean the room.
  • clean the room before.
  • write a letter.
  • write a letter five days ago.
  • study English.
  • study English ten days ago.
  • watch TV.
  • watch TV five weeks ago.
  • play tennis.
  • play tennis ten weeks ago.

wash my face [세수를 하다] make coffee [커피를 타다] eat breakfast [아침 식사를 하다] drink juice [쥬스를 마시다] clean the room [방을 청소하다] read the book [책을 읽다] write a letter [편지를 쓰다] study English [영어를 공부하다] watch TV [TV를 보다] play tennis [테니스를 치다]
